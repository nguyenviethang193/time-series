In reporting the results of your analysis, you should discuss how they relate to the literature.

Suggested Structure:

1. Introduction (about 2 pages)
    a. Identify your research question
    b. Explain the significance of the question
2. Literature Review & Background (about 3 - 4 pages)
    a. Provide background information or context to set out your research
    b. Summarize what has been studied in your area (what we know already and
       what we still don’t know that’s worth knowing)
    c. Explain how your research relates to existing studies
3. Methods (about 1- 2 pages)
    a. Describe the data

    c. Describe the statistical techniques that you used for your analysis
